Bio

Over the past few years I have been working towards fulfilling my ambition to becoming a software engineer. During my first few years at Stony Brook, I had no idea what my passion was. I ended up studying chemistry because I understood it really well and believed it to be promising.

After working as a chemist for a few years, I realized that was certainly not what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. I eventually came across the wonderful world of programming by taking a few introductory courses online. I was drawn to the freedom of creativity, open ended problem solving, and the satisfaction of overcoming challenges.

I returned to school to obtain my second B.S. in Computer Science from Oregon State University. During this time, I continued to work as a chemist until I received an internship as a software engineer at PSEG Long Island. I am now searching for full-time employment to finally have a career doing what I truly enjoy.
